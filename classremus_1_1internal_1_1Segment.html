Detailed Description

A Segment object to represent a slab of RDMA memory.

A contiguous region of remotely accessible memory. Size is always a power of 2, and it's always aligned to its size. The interface is really just "raw pointer". If you want something more complex (e.g., an allocator inside of it), you need to make it yourself.
